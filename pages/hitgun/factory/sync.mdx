# Sync

Enables integration of the target video into the generated vertical video.

When checked:

- **Main Video Position**
- **Main Video Ratio**
- **Merged Area**

options appear.

## Main Video Position

The main video represents the generated vertical video.

There are two options in the main video position: **Top** and **Bottom**.

By default, the Top option is checked, and the main video is positioned at the top in the generated content.

When the Main Video Position is checked as Bottom, the merged video moves to the top, and the main video is positioned at the bottom.


## Main Video Ratio

The main video ratio indicates the portion of the screen occupied by the main video in the split screen.

By default, this ratio is 70. The number 70 represents 70%.

The remaining 30% section contains the Merged video.



## Merged Area

Specifies the area covered by the merged video.

There are two options: **Default (%100)** and **Advanced**.

### Default (%100)

The entire target video is integrated.

### Advanced

If you want to integrate a specific portion of the target video instead of the entire video, you can use the advanced option.

When the **Advanced** option is checked, two options named **Percent** and **Pixel** appear as **Merged Custom Area Type**.
